Frequently Asked Questions

Who is Hatsune Miku?

Hatsune Miku, codename CV01, was the first Japanese VOCALOID to be both developed and distributed by Crypton Future Media, Inc. Her voice is provided by the Japanese voice actress Saki Fujita.

What is VOCALOID?

Vocaloid is a singing synthesizer application software developed by the YAMAHA Corporation.he project was an international effort, and is considered the brainchild of Kenmochi Hideki, also known as the "father" of VOCALOID.

Where can I find this software?

You can find this software on the official VOCALOID website

You can also find copies of the software on secondhand on websites like e-bay.
